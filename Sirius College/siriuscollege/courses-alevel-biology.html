Biology

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course.

Entry requirements

Students must have a minimum of 5 A*-C GCSE qualifications including English and Maths and they are required to have two Science GCSE qualifications at a minimum of grade B.

What will I learn?

Biology is one of the most popular A Level subjects in the country, attracting students studying a wide range of other subjects. The AS qualification is studied over one year with the A level qualification taking two years.

A level Biology builds on the concepts and skills that were gained throughout the GCSE course and presents Biology as exciting, challenging and relevant to our everyday lives as well as allowing the opportunities to investigate new areas.

Within this subject you will study:

Biological molecules

Cells

Organisms exchange substances with their environment

Genetic information, variation and relationshipsbetween organisms
