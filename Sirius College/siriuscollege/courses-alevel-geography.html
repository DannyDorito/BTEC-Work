Geography

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course.

Entry requirements

You will need a minimum of five GCSE’s at grade D or above, including English Language. You must have a keen interest in the Travel and Tourism industry and be willing to attend work experience placements during the year.

What will I learn?

You will learn the mandatory topics which are:

The UK travel and tourism sector.

Understanding customer service in travel and tourism.

Understanding the nature and effects of world travel.

You will also look at a range of other topics to make up the 60 credits in units such as:

UK tourism destinations, holiday planning and introduction to cruise operations.
