History

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course.

Entry requirements

You will need a minimum of a Grade B in GCSE History and at least five GCSEs at grade A*-C including Maths and English.

What will I learn?

This course will allow you to study history from over a period of at least 200 years. It will enable you to broaden your historical knowledge and strengthen your skills of evaluation and interpretation. You will examine a wide range of historical sources and historians’ interpretations allowing you to reach your own informed conclusions. You will have the opportunity to take part in visits to places of historical significance to further broaden your understanding.

Within this course you will study a range of historical periods and people including:

Henry VII

Henry VIII

International relations during the Cold War

Civil Rights
