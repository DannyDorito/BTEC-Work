Law

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course.

Entry requirements

At least 5 GCSEs at grades A* - C including Maths and English are required but it is imperative that you can express yourself clearly in writing, as such, a minimum of Grade B in English is desirable.

What will I learn?

This A-Level course will give you an opportunity to develop an understanding of Law and the Legal System. You will study Criminal and one of Contract or Tort Law.

Studying Law students will gain logic, debate and critical analysis skills, all of which will enable them to progress to University.

A legal qualification is widely sought-after and an indication to employers/university admissions personnel of the intelligence and dedication of the student.

It will give you a real-life understanding of legal rules, you will be able to understand the reasons why people are arrested and possible defences that may arise.

Within this subject you will study:

Unit 1 – Law Making and the Legal System (Creation of Law)

Unit 2 – Criminal Law/Tort Law (Assault, ABH, GBH and Murder) (Personal Injury, Negligent Death)

Unit 3 – Murder/Manslaughter

Unit 4 – Topics of Law – Is Law Just?
