Art & Design

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course. A successful candidate will achieve a qualification equivalent to 3 A Levels at grades A*-C.

Entry requirements

You will need 5 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-C including Maths and English and will have ideally completed GCSE or L2 BTEC Art & Design.

What will I learn?

We have fantastic studio facilities including a Mac suite, Ceramics and Textiles studios, planned trips to London and Europe, industry links and experienced staff with outstanding knowledge, understanding and experience of the Art and Design Industry.

Students will gain experience in working with ‘live’ clients and have a developed understanding of the Art and Design industry. Students will have weekly group sessions and one to one tutorials to discuss progress. Coursework forms 100% of the qualification.
