Business

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course. A successful candidate will achieve a qualification equivalent to 3 A Levels at grades A*-C.

Entry requirements

You will need 5 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-C including Maths and English and ideally have completed GCSE Business or L2 BTEC Business.

What will I learn?

This is an exciting course where you will learn about the world of business and enterprise and gain essential skills for success in your future career, whether that means working in an existing business or starting your own. You will have opportunities to set up your own enterprise and business ventures on site.

Within this subject you will study:

Business Communication

Creative Product Promotion

Internet Marketing in Business

Visual Merchandising in Retail

Website Design Strategy

Starting a Small Business
