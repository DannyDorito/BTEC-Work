Hairdressing

Length of course

This is a full time, one year course. A successful candidate will achieve a VRQ Level 2 Diploma.

Entry requirements

You will need 4 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-D including English.

What will I learn?

This is an exciting opportunity to work in a live salon environment, gain a Level 2 qualification and also work one day a week in industry. All equipment is provided and stored at the academy so students don’t have to carry a heavy kit. Textbook and uniform are provided free of charge to all students. There are planned visits to industry events.

Within this subject you will study:

Working in the hair industry

Client consultation

Shampoo and conditioning the hair and scalp

Cutting women’s hair

Cutting men’s hair

Colour and lighten hair

Salon reception duties
