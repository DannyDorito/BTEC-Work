Information & Technology

Length of course

This is a full-time, two year course. Successful candidates will achieve a qualification that is equivalent to three A Levels at grades A – C.

Entry requirements

You will need 5 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-C including ICT, Mathematics and English. You will also enjoy working with computers and have an interest in technology.

What will I learn?

The course is designed to be challenging, exciting and stimulating. It will give you the knowledge, understanding and skills you will need for employment in the IT industry or for further study

There are six mandatory units:

Communication and Employability Skills for IT

Computer Systems

Information Systems

Software Design and Development

Systems Analysis and Design

Event Driven Programming

In addition, there are these specialist units:

Managing Networks

Computer Networks

IT Technical Support

IT Systems Troubleshooting and Repair

Object Oriented Programming

Procedural Programming

Developing Computer Games

Maintaining Computer Systems

Website Production

Installing and Upgrading Software

Digital Graphics

Computer Animation
