Performing Arts

Length of course

2 years (3 x A Level equivalent)

Entry requirements

5 GCSEs at grades C and above, including English. Alternatively, BTEC Level 2 Diploma in Performing Arts or other related performance qualifications. Performance experience is desirable as students will be expected to perform in public.

What will I learn?

You will learn about different types and styles of theatre and develop your acting and performance skills in practical contexts, including public performances. Lessons will be mainly practical and you will work collaboratively with other students in the group to research, rehearse and perform both devised and scripted work.

We have superb purpose-built facilities including drama, dance, music and recording studios and multi-functional indoor and outdoor theatre spaces. You will take part in regular and diverse theatre trips, as well as workshops with visiting professional practitioners and theatre companies. Staff delivering the course have strong links with the professional industry and are able to give relevant careers advice to anyone hoping to pursue a career in the performing arts.

The BTEC Diploma is a nationally recognised and respected qualification which provides a direct route into degree courses in acting, musical theatre, performing arts or Drama School training.
