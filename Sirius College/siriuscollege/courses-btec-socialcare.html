Health & Social Care

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course. A successful candidate will achieve a qualification equivalent to 3 A Levels.

Entry requirements

You will need 5 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-C including Maths and English.

What will I learn?

The BTEC Extended National Diploma in Health and Social Care will provide students with an understanding of the skills required for work in this sector. Students will benefit from gaining work experience in the different sectors; which will help aid career choices. Students will spend one day a week on placement experiencing three different settings.

Students will be taught by a former nurse and specialists in anatomy and physiology and sociology. They will have access to a wide range of placements including care homes, primary schools, special schools and nurseries.

Within this subject you will study:

Developing Effective Communication in Health and Social Care

Health, Safety and Security in Health and Social Care

Development Through the Life Stages

Personal and Professional Development in Health and Social Care

Sociological Perspectives in Health and Social Care

Psychological Perspectives in Health and Social Care
