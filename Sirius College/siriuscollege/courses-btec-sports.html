Sports

Length of course

This is a full time, two year course. A successful candidate will achieve a qualification equivalent to 3 A Levels at grades A*-C.

Entry requirements

You will need 5 GCSEs or equivalent at grades A*-C including Maths and English and ideally have completed GCSE PE or L2 BTEC Sport.

What will I learn?

This course will give you knowledge and practical experience of the sports industry in areas like injury, fitness testing and training, psychology and talent identification. You will be part of the Coaching Academy, allowing you to develop as a leader and a coach, gaining nationally recognised qualifications as you progress. You will take on numerous roles within the PE department and local sports industry including officiating, coaching and performance analysis. Students on the course take part in numerous visits including outdoor and adventurous activities at Welton Waters, fitness testing at Hull University and camping at Dalby Forest

Within this subject you will study:

Biological molecules

Sports Coaching

Practical Sport

Fitness Training & Programming

Fitness Testing

Sports Nutrition

Psychology for Sports Performance

Analysis of Sports Performance and Officiating in Sport

What they think

Declan Smith was one of six students who achieved the top grade available (D*D*D*) and he progressed onto Leeds Beckett University to study Sports Development.

Previous Sirius sports students have progressed on to university to study degrees in Primary and Secondary Teaching, Sports Rehabilitation, Sports Development and Sports Coaching. This qualification is well respected by employers and apprenticeship providers for those not looking to go down the university route.
